A few reasons why our valued customers choose us.

Plain and simple! Our team & technlogy curate the best experiences in your city at your desired member level. Ranging from classes, comedy, concerts, dining, movies, sports, wellness, etc., we are sure that you will be satisfied with every event every month.

FAQ

If you don't see an answer to your question, you can send us an email.

We offer a tiered monthly subscription service that randomly selects an event in your city every month based on dates of your choosing. Depending on your tier, you will be sent 2-5 tickets monthly.

The Monthly Memory uses complex algorithms to make sure our customers are getting the most value for every dollar! Our technology is engineered to make sure that every member is receiving the best ticket possible for the best events in their city. We curate event lists and make sure that we find the best ticket value for each member. We refuse to include events in our collection that are not high-value and proven.

We give all our members a month to plan their schedule ahead and make sure they have free days to attend their mystery event. In the event that you have to cancel last minute, we will do everything we can to provide credits that can be applied to a future event.

We hope that you will be excited about every pair of tickets you are awarded. In the event you are not happy with your draw, every member will get 1 re-spin attempt every month. You must re-spin right after your first spin attempt. Our first 100 customers will receive 3 free re-spin attempts every month with a lifetime guarantee.

We are offering a credit system. In the event that you want to take a break from events for a month without pausing your subscription, we will roll that exact dollar credits to your profile for future events.

After you spin the wheel for your specified date, it will land on one of the 8 categories. Our technology will interpret your event category and immediately notify you of the specific event you have been awarded.
